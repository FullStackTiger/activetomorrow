About Richard

I’ve been doing real estate marketing since 2014. In that time, I’ve learned that agents need a reliable partner who produce great work, quickly.

Currently, I’m studying to get my BFA at Arizona State. Photography and architecture have been life-long passions of mine. I love that working in this field affords me the opportunity to combine these two powerful mediums. I love meeting new people, exploring new spaces, and creating meaningful imagery of them.

I would love for the opportunity to become your “go-to” real estate listing photographer.
